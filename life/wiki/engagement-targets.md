# Engagement Targets

Curated accounts to monitor for daily engagement (replies on Twitter, comments on LinkedIn). Goal: ASTEROI inbound + personal brand visibility among DTC operators.

The `/engage` command reads this file, pulls last-24h posts from these accounts via Apify, scores them for reply opportunity, and outputs a daily briefing of 5-8 posts worth your time.

**Voice:** Juan writes all replies himself. This system is discovery + curation only.

---

## Groups

Three groups, each with a different strategic purpose:

| Group | Purpose | Reply style |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **ASTEROI ICPs** | DTC founders / growth leads who fit ASTEROI's client profile. Goal: be visible to them, demonstrate competence, open DMs. | Specific, useful, no pitch. Share a number or test result from Mars Men. |
| **Peers** | Other operators, agency folks, growth leads in your orbit. Goal: build network, be known among peers. | Direct, opinionated. Engage with the substance, not the surface. |
| **Inspiration** | People whose thinking you absorb. Goal: learning + occasional visibility-by-association. | Lower priority. Reply only when you have something real to add. |

## Signal Filters

What makes a post worth surfacing. The `/engage` command uses these to score.

### Reply-worthy signals (boost score)
- Author asks a genuine question (invites reply, conversation more likely)
- Author shares a specific number / test result (you can respond with your own data)
- Contrarian or counter-intuitive take (room for nuance, not just "+1")
- Post is < 12h old (still in the algorithm window, replies still get seen)
- Engagement-to-followers ratio is high (mid-virality = your reply gets seen)

### Skip signals (suppress score)
- Generic motivational / quote posts (no substance to engage with)
- Self-promotional product launches (replies look like noise)
- Polls (low reply ROI)
- Posts already > 100 replies (your reply gets buried)
- Posts > 24h old (algorithm window closed)

### Topic priorities (boost score)
Topics Juan has real ground to stand on:
- CAC / unit economics / profitability at scale
- Post-purchase / LTV / subscription
- Creative testing systems
- DTC operator's view of agency relationships
- Building internal tools / AI for ops

### Topic deprioritizations (suppress score)
- AI prompt engineering generic content
- Crypto, no-code SaaS, motivational founder content
- Pure brand / design content (not Juan's lane)

## Daily Quota

Juan's stated volume: **20-40 replies/day total across both platforms**.

- **Briefing size:** surface ~40-50 candidates so Juan picks 20-40 worth replying to (some posts won't survive review)
- **Cadence:** Once per morning, covers last 24h
- **Quota split:** ~25 Twitter + ~10-15 LinkedIn — Twitter has higher reply velocity, LinkedIn each comment carries more weight per ICP
- **Time budget:** at 1-2 min per Twitter reply + 3-5 min per LinkedIn comment, this is 60-90 min/day. Plan for it.
