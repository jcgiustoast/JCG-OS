## Observations for content strategy

1. **Lead magnets dominate reach.** The three 50+ like posts are all gated downloads (Andromeda guide, P&L model, subscription checklist). Regular takes sit 3–20 likes. Continue seeding lead magnets monthly.
2. **ROAS/incrementality is the signature take.** Roughly 11 of 50 posts rework this theme. It's the most repeatable hook — safe to keep hammering.
3. **Short one-liners underperform.** Posts under 100 chars (headlines without body) consistently score 3–10 likes. Either add body copy or cut.
4. **Spanish-only archive.** Phase 1 content-strategy prescribes English-first — this archive is the pre-Phase-1 corpus and should be treated as raw material to translate/adapt for English channels, not re-posted as-is.
5. **Finance-first framing outperforms tactics-only.** Posts that anchor in P&L / contribution margin / cash flow (e.g. "quiebra no es el P&L", "finanzas + estrategia > IA") pull more engagement than pure Meta Ads tactics.
