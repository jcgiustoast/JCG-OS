# Blog (jcgiusto.com)

## Platform DNA

- Long-form, SEO-bearing, evergreen
- 1,500-3,000 words per article
- Juan's personal hub — framework-heavy, math-heavy, framework-naming titles
- Audience: [[operators]] and [[founders]] searching for solutions to specific problems
- Outlives every social post — this is where the body of work compounds

## Phase 1 Firewall (see [[content-strategy]])

- No Mars Men by name; credential usage allowed as "Head of eCommerce at a 9-figure subscription brand"
- No Meta Ads tactics, creative strategy, ad spend
- No ASTEROI service CTAs
- Published as Juan's personal brand, not agency content

## Structure

1. **Title:** Framework-naming or contrarian claim. Pattern: "The [Framework Name]: [What it does]" or "[Contrarian claim]." See [[hooks]].
2. **Opening (100-200 words):** Scenario hook or contrarian premise. Establish the problem. Often an Economics Reveal.
3. **The problem explained (300-500 words):** Why the current approach fails. Math-driven.
4. **The framework (500-1,000 words):** Juan's replacement approach. Named framework. Include formulas in inline code, diagrams described in text, specific numbers.
5. **How to implement (300-500 words):** Concrete steps. "If you have X, do Y." Actionable.
6. **Conclusion (100-200 words):** Restate the core insight. No service CTA — the framework IS the value.

## Content Rules

- Voice per [[brand-voice]], adapted per [[platform-tone]] (most structured, most rigorous)
- Subheadings every 200-400 words
- Bold key phrases for scannability
- Include comparison tables (reported vs reality, before vs after)
- Inline formulas: `MARGINAL CONTRIBUTION = Revenue - Variable Costs - Ad Spend`
- Reference other articles/frameworks by name for internal linking — see existing pieces in [[subscription-lab]]
- No author bio CTA

## Posting Strategy

- Frequency: 1 deep piece/month (Phase 1)
- SEO-optimized title (primary keyword, natural phrasing, under 60 chars)
- Meta description = first 160 chars of the Economics Reveal or framework promise

## Repurposing Notes

- In Juan's [[repurpose]] chain, blog is often the **seed** when the piece is framework-heavy — the Economics Reveal needs math room before compressing.
- After publishing: extract one framework into a LinkedIn long post, one Economics Reveal into a Twitter thread, one contrarian claim into a Twitter single.
- Link back from social to the blog as the canonical reference.
- See [[repurpose]] for the full chain.
