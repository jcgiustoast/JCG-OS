## PRINCIPLE 1: SEE CLEAR

*"Unified real-time metrics dashboard"*

### The Problem That Kills Profitability: Fragmented Data

If you feel like your business data is scattered across ten different platforms, you're not alone.

73% of eCommerce executives make critical decisions with incomplete or outdated information.

The absence of a "single source of truth" condemns you to navigate blindly. And no, the CFO's monthly reports don't count. By the time they arrive, it's already too late to correct course.

### The Solution: Unified Real-Time Dashboard

eCommerce businesses that dominate their market centralize **all** critical metrics in a single source of truth:

**Operational Metrics:**

* Sales by channel and product
* Advertising spend with granular breakdown
* Real costs (product, logistics, commissions)

**Profitability Metrics:**

* Margins by product line
* Real marginal contribution
* MER (Marketing Efficiency Ratio)

**Growth Metrics:**

* New vs. recurring customers
* AOV and LTV by cohort
* ROAS and CAC per channel

**The result:** Decisions based on real data, not estimates. Aligned teams. Discrepancies eliminated.

## PRINCIPLE 2: PLAY SMART

*"Strategic research to identify winning opportunities"*

### The Golden Age of eCommerce Is Over

Before it was simple: products from China + Facebook advertising = thousands in daily sales.

Today you face a brutal reality:

* CPMs 300% more expensive than in 2020
* Competition emerging weekly
* Increasingly unpredictable algorithms
* SEO being devoured by AI

**The level of sophistication required to win today is exponentially higher.**

### The Difference: Strategy Based on Deep Analysis

Every sustainable competitive advantage begins with meticulous research. Once you have clarity about your current situation, you need analysis that reveals actionable insights:

**Product Research:**

* Which products generate the highest LTV with the lowest CAC
* Differentiation opportunities vs. direct competition

**Channel Research:**

* Under-exploited channels with scalability potential
* Geographic markets with low entry barriers

**Competitive Research:**

* Winning strategies you can adapt or surpass
* Market gaps you can exploit

**The framework:** "Where we want to play" + "How we want to win" + Specific actions to dominate.

Combine internal data with competitive intelligence to create strategies that systematically exploit hidden advantages.

## PRINCIPLE 3: BUILD THE MODEL

*"Precise forecasting that guides your team's actions"*

### A Brilliant Strategy Without Execution Is Worthless

You can have the best strategy in the world, but if you don't translate it into concrete, measurable objectives for every team member, execution will be mediocre.

### The Forecast as an Execution Tool

An effective forecast is not just prediction -- it's an execution engine that pushes your team toward constant creativity and optimization.

**It must be:**

* **Ambitious** to maintain motivation and creative thinking
* **Data-based** on historical data, market trends, and external variables
* **Granular** down to the daily level to generate immediate action

**Critical Forecast Components:**

**Revenue & Growth:**

* Revenue by channel and product
* Expected new and recurring customers
* Inventory and demand projection

**Investment & Returns:**

* Advertising spend optimized by channel
* Expected profitability by business line
* Projected ROI per marketing initiative

**The result:** A common objective that transforms your team into a coordinated execution machine.

## PRINCIPLE 4: PROFIT FIRST

*"Real profitability as the North Star for all decisions"*

### Growing at Any Cost Is Officially Dead

AllBirds, Casper, Dollar Shave Club... Giants that promised to dominate the world and are now shadows of what they were. What killed them?

They acquired customers at a loss expecting an LTV that never arrived. They believed the 2017 CPMs would last forever. **The result: millions in losses and broken promises.**

### The Winning Approach: Obsession with Marginal Contribution

eCommerce businesses that build sustainable empires have **total obsession** with marginal contribution after **ALL** real costs:

**Costs Included:**

* Product and logistics (obvious but poorly calculated)
* Direct and indirect marketing
* Operations and real overhead
* Prorated fixed costs

**On their dashboard, the main metric is marginal contribution.** Everything else is subordinate.

**Their methodology:**

1. They start the forecast with the net profit target
2. They apply reverse engineering to determine required revenue
3. They evaluate every decision by its impact on net profitability
4. They prioritize sustainable growth over speed

**Frugality is a fundamental value, not a constraint.**

They understand that the ultimate goal is not to reach arbitrary revenue figures -- it's to generate real profits for shareholders.

## PRINCIPLE 5: EXECUTE SYSTEMS

*"Intelligent automation + daily actions that guarantee results"*

### Without Execution Systems, Even the Best Strategy Becomes Improvisation

If you've implemented the first four principles but lack automated frameworks and specific daily actions, you're inviting chaos and discoordination.

### Automated Decision Frameworks

**Launch System:**

* Specific number of campaigns (Meta, Google, TikTok) per month
* Email and SMS schedule for seasonal campaigns
* Automatic triggers based on performance

**Correction System:**

* Predefined actions when marginal contribution falls below target
* Automatic escalations by channel
* Spending limits and optimization rules

### Example of a System in Action

**Situation:** Day 15, marginal contribution 20% below target.

**Automatic Analysis:**

1. Revenue on track
2. Excessive advertising spend (warning)
3. Inefficiency in new customer acquisition (warning)
4. Specific Facebook campaign: spend 200%, ROAS -50% (warning)

**Immediate Actions:**

1. Adjust bid limits on the problematic campaign
2. Create an alternative campaign with a new audience
3. Redistribute budget toward channels with better performance

**As you can see:** With clear metrics, defined objectives, and action systems, every day you know exactly what to do to stay on track toward your monthly goals.

**You eliminate improvisation. You guarantee consistent execution.**
