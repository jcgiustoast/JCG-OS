# The Origin of ASTEROI: From Personal Failure to an eCommerce Growth Revolution

## Introduction

**Juan Cruz Giusto**
June 26, 2025

_When I Lost My Money Chasing False Metrics_

## Year 2020: The eCommerce Boom in Argentina

I came to Spain to live near my then-girlfriend. As a padel fanatic, I had arrived in the best place in the world for this sport.

When I got into the scene, I saw that "innovative" products were being sold here that didn't even exist in Argentina: ball pressurizers, silicone grips, vibration dampeners.

Motivated and eager to learn, I contacted a great friend with a degree in international trade:

**"We have to bring these products to Argentina no matter what."**

I had checked MercadoLibre -- the Amazon of Latin America -- and there wasn't a single one. Zero competition. It was a clear opportunity.

## The Investment and the Dream

I invested more than half my savings in buying products from China and shipping them by boat to Argentina. The process took almost a year and many thousands of dollars coming out of my account.

**September 2021:** I created the website with WordPress, designed the images in Canva, and recorded videos promoting the product. With high expectations, we activated advertising on Facebook and Google.

I was constantly checking WooCommerce reports to see if we had generated the first sale.

After a few hours, it appeared.

I contacted my partner and we started talking about what we were going to do when we sold the company for $1M.

**Every time we increased advertising, sales increased.**

We ended up spending our salary on advertising in just a few days.

We had even thought about quitting our jobs to focus 100% on our eCommerce business.

## Reality Hits Hard

Months later, we ran out of stock on our star product.

When I started making the bank transfer for the new purchase, I realized that we had just enough to buy slightly more than what we had initially brought in.

It seemed strange to me.

I decided to review the numbers in detail.

**And that's when I discovered the brutal reality:**

* 90% of sales came from one product, but the others barely moved
* Shipping costs were much higher than expected
* MercadoLibre charged a high commission (30%) that ate into the margin

**All those previously hidden costs destroyed our profitability.**

We decided on a second purchase, but while the products were traveling across the Pacific, competition appeared.

They started selling almost the same thing -- only the color changed -- at 30% cheaper.

**Advertising was no longer profitable. There was no margin to cover acquisition costs.**

My hopes of becoming a millionaire died while I was deactivating Meta campaigns.

## The Moment of Clarity: Seeing the Same Mistake at a Scale of Millions

Years later, having learned that understanding finance is key to doing marketing, I began working at one of the most important CRO agencies in Spain.

I worked with the country's largest eCommerce brands, running experiments that generated up to 15% conversion rate lifts.

**But when we talked about business beyond the tests, it reminded me of my situation in Argentina:**

* Money parked in hard-to-sell inventory
* High Meta ROAS that wasn't reflected in the bank account
* Costs eating into margins
* New competition appearing every two weeks

The scale was different -- they were billing millions while we were barely doing hundreds of thousands -- but **the root of the problem was the same:**

_We were doing marketing without a financial foundation or strategy behind it._

## The Exact Moment That Changed Everything

The breaking point came during a monthly results meeting with a client.

A CMO from a brand billing EUR 8M annually was presenting the numbers:

> "We have a ROAS of 4.0, sales went up 15%, all KPIs are green..."

But when the CFO presented the financial numbers, the reality was different: profitability had worsened.

The tension was palpable.

The founder was asking how it was possible that with so much marketing "success" there wasn't more money in the bank.

The CMO had no answer.

**That's when I saw it clear as day.**

It wasn't just my story. It was the story of almost every eCommerce business I had known.

To make matters worse, I repeatedly witnessed agencies calling themselves "marketing experts," manipulating ROAS and trying to demonstrate good work when every euro in advertising was losing money.

**In the land of the blind, the one-eyed man is king.**

Someone had to connect marketing with finance. Someone had to teach that the goal is not to grow at any cost, but to put real money in the bank account.

**That day, ASTEROI was born.**

## The Mission: Ending the Roller Coaster of Growing Without Earning

My mission is to give financial clarity and marketing strategy to eCommerce businesses billing EUR 1-10M annually.

To connect marketing and finance teams so they work aligned, instead of fighting each month in results meetings.

To help founders and CMOs turn their eCommerce into a predictable profit-generating machine, where every euro invested in marketing translates into real money in the bank account.

**When that happens, everything changes:**

* **CMOs:** Regain confidence knowing every action generates real return
* **Founders:** Have certainty that their eCommerce is putting money in their account
* **Teams:** The fights end and everyone works under the same strategy

This is only possible when marketing, finance, and strategy are combined.

## The Vision: Changing the Rules of the Game

I have witnessed the radical change that occurs when eCommerce leaders gain clarity about their situation: **growth rates that double, margins that increase, happy and aligned teams.**

My vision is to make this the norm, not the exception.

> I want a future where no founder loses money chasing false metrics like it happened to me.

Where growth is always synonymous with real profitability. Where marketing and finance work as a single team. Where fundamental principles replace tactics that change every quarter.

**My fundamental shift is simple:** prioritize profitability above everything. Do marketing with a solid financial foundation.

When Growth agencies focus on putting real money in the client's bank account -- month after month, predictably -- the industry will have changed forever.

**And ASTEROI is going to lead that change.**

## Conclusion

Without a financial understanding of the business, a solid strategy, and a daily action system, marketing teams are blind and misaligned.

But when everything is combined -- financial clarity, coherent strategy, and systematic execution -- the result is inevitable: a business that grows year after year with a sense of control that previously seemed impossible.

**Are you ready to turn your eCommerce into a real profit-generating machine?**
