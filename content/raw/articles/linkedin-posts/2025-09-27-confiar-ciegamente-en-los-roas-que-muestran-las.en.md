Blindly trusting the ROAS ad platforms report back to you is easy.

The numbers look spectacular. Too spectacular to be real.

But when you open your sales report, the revenue curve hasn't moved. And your margin keeps shrinking.

There's a world outside of what Meta, Google and TikTok tell you. One where you can actually know what your marketing spend is doing.

Incrementality tests. MMM. Causal inference.

Those are the tools the platforms can't inflate or game. Once you implement them, you unlock profitability and growth ceilings you didn't know you had.

The question is: are you ready to take the red pill? Or would you rather let Meta and Google keep telling you what you want to hear?
