With Meta Andromeda, the rules of the game in eCommerce have changed.

If you're still running the 2024 playbook, you're falling behind.

Here are the 8 tactics you need to implement NOW to get the most out of Andromeda:

**1. Increase creative volume.**

Before Andromeda, Meta couldn't process more than 5–10 creatives per ad set. Now it can analyze up to 50.

If you were making 5 a week, find a way to make 20 or 50. Look at the Ads Library of the eComs that are actually winning — some brands have up to 1,000 ads running.

**2. Make sure the volume is diverse.**

"Volume" is not 20 iterations of the same image with a different background color.

Meta detects whether a creative is genuinely different or just a tweak.

You need real diversity across:
- Product angles
- Problems addressed
- Formats (UGC, stop motion, product photography)
- Video lengths
- Copy and hooks

**3. Stop wasting time on manual targeting.**

If you're still segmenting by interests and lookalikes, you are putting sticks in the algorithm's wheels.

Keep the audience as broad as possible — only gender, age, and geography.

The creative *is* the targeting. Not your manual setup.

**4. Consolidate campaigns and ad sets.**

Meta recommends at least 20 different creatives per ad set, up to 50.

If your ads talk about the same product and target the same person, they belong in the same ad set. It's the fastest way to exit the learning phase.

**5. Use Meta's AI creative tools.**

Yes, sometimes it adds strange music. Adds elements your creative team didn't design. Changes background colors.

Meta claims it performs better: +11% CTR and +7.6% conversion rate. And logically, they have no reason to lie here.

**6. Improve your site and customer journey.**

With Sequence Learning, Meta factors in what happens *inside* your site to rank your ads.

Invest in:
- Page speed
- CRO
- A frictionless purchase experience
- Customer service

Without this, your ads will eventually stop working.

**7. Let the algorithm do its job.**

Sound familiar: *You launch a new ad set with a €20 CPA target. Two days in, CPA is €50, so you cut the budget.*

Mistake.

Meta has millions of data points. You don't. Don't assume your judgment beats the algorithm.

Learn to trust it. Let it optimize. Focus on strategy.

**8. (Implicit: the other seven force this one.)** Shift your time away from Ads Manager micro-management and into strategy, creative briefing, and offer design — that's where the leverage is now.
