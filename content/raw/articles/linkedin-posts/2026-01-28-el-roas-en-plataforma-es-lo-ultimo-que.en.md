Platform ROAS is the last thing you should look at in your day-to-day.

There are dozens of metrics that matter more in your eCommerce:

- Marginal Contribution
- Net Revenue
- MER
- aMER
- CAC
- Return Rate
- 30/60/90-day LTV
- Refund Rate
- ARPU
- AOV
- Conversion Rate

And I could keep going with at least 10 more.

The ROAS you see inside Meta and Google is an estimate — inflated in most cases — that doesn't reflect the reality of your eCommerce.

It only makes sense when read inside a specific context: the strategy and the financials behind the business.

Without that context, it's usually a mirage that leads nowhere.
