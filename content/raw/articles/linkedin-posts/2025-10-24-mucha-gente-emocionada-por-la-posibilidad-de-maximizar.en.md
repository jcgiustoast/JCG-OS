A lot of people are excited about "Maximize ROAS" on Meta.

In my view, using it in eCommerce is a mistake.

"Maximize" sounds great, especially inside Ads Manager. Who doesn't want to generate the most sales possible, or the highest ROAS?

But one component is missing: at what ROAS?

With this strategy, Meta will push ROAS as high as possible — but that number is often higher than your business can actually pay for. The result: campaigns that generate losses.

Let me rewrite the strategy description more honestly:

*"We'll do our best to acquire customers profitably, but if we can't, no problem — we'll keep spending."*

More honest, right?

Luckily, Cost Caps exist.

If you don't know what that is, I explained it in another post — link in comments.
