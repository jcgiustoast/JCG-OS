If your eCommerce isn't growing profitably, you're optimizing for the wrong metric.

After analyzing 7- and 8-figure eCommerce brands, I found a clear pattern.

The ones that scale sustainably vs. the ones that stall have ONE key difference:

**They understand the correct metric hierarchy.**

The 4-level framework:

**Level 1 — Financial metrics**
- Marginal Contribution (your North Star)
- Net Profit
- The metrics closest to your bank account

**Level 2 — Business metrics**
- Net revenue
- MER (Marketing Efficiency Ratio)
- The ones that reveal overall health

**Level 3 — Customer metrics**
- New vs. returning
- CAC and First Order Margin
- The ones that protect the future

**Level 4 — Platform metrics**
- ROAS on Meta, Google, TikTok
- CPA by platform
- The metrics furthest from your financial reality

The paradox to understand:

Lower level = more control, less precision.
Higher level = less control, more financial precision.

The winning strategy: monitor all levels, but decide from the top down.
