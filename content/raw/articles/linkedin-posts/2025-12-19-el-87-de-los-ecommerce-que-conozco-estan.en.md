87% of the eCommerce brands I know are optimizing AOV for ghosts.

Not a joke.

They pull AOV from their dashboard, design their entire ticket-size strategy around that number… and the metric never moves.

Because that "average customer" who spends €67 per order doesn't exist.

If you do €1M–€30M in revenue and you've been making decisions based on AOV, this is going to sting.

But it's also going to help you build strategies that unlock thousands of euros.
