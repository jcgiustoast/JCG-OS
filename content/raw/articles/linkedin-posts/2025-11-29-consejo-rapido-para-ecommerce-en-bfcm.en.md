Quick tip for eCommerce brands running BFCM:

Set your Meta campaigns to 1-day click attribution.

You need to force the algorithm to drive the purchase now, not 7 days from now.

It's also the best way to make decisions in real time and stay effective during the window.

And don't even get me started on 1-day view — the least incremental setting of all. Stay as far away from it as possible.

Good luck with the 3 days left.
