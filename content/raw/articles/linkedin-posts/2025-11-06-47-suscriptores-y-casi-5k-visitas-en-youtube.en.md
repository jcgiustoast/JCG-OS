47 subscribers and nearly 5K views on YouTube.

Feels small. But every channel that is huge today started exactly here — MrBeast, The Nude Project, Romuald Fons, all of them.

That's why I think calibrating expectations matters. If I had expected thousands of subscribers in 3 months of work, the channel would already be dead.

I know this is a marathon, not a sprint. Results take time to show up — in YouTube, in eCommerce, and in basically anything worth doing.

So, eyes on the long game. Enjoy the process. Improve every week. The results come on their own.
