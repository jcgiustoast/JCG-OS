A good eCommerce forecast isn't about predicting the most likely future.

It's the one that sets targets for the whole team that are ambitious but achievable.

It's the one that makes you think differently, look for non-obvious solutions, and forces you to improve.

A compass that points the way — not a crystal ball playing at divination.

If your 2026 forecast feels more like tarot than a strategic plan, revisit it.
