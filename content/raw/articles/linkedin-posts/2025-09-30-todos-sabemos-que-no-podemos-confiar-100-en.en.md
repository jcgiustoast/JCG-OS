We all know we can't trust Meta, Google and TikTok at 100%.

It's like asking a kid to grade his own homework and expecting an honest answer. Most of the time — brand campaigns are the clearest case — platforms inflate their own results.

The good news: there's a way to know the real impact of your campaigns and make decisions based on data and experimentation.

Incrementality tests.

If you want to learn what they are and how to implement them in your eCommerce, my long-form write-up is in the comments.
