Let's talk about the forgotten heroes of eCommerce.

And why you need to work with these two departments to grow.

In Growth, we sometimes get too full of ourselves. I've been guilty of it.

We assume that if we generate more sales, every other department will happily follow us into the millions.

But for that to actually happen, two teams have to be aligned and ready for the growth. **Ops and Finance.**

**Ops** needs a forecast of what you'll sell and when, so they can purchase inventory in time. Suppliers are often overseas, and sea freight can take months. If you run out of stock, your sales go to zero.

**Finance** needs cash on hand to pay for ad campaigns, for inventory, and sometimes for the extra production capacity you need to meet demand. Without a proper cash flow plan, Finance can't fund the growth.

As Growth Managers, Strategists, and Heads of Growth, we have to learn to work hand in hand with both. They are the ones who enable the numbers we're chasing.

Make friends with them. They tend to be pretty nice, too.
