ROAS tanking in your eCommerce before BFCM?

The last thing you should do is cut ad spend.

Let me explain.

November is a completely different animal from the rest of the year.

Instead of seeing an ad and buying, people add products to a wishlist and wait for the Black Friday discount.

It doesn't matter if you've pulled your promotions forward to last the whole month. People don't believe it, and most of them will still wait.

So if you cut spend on Meta, Google, or YouTube — especially top-of-funnel channels — because of weak performance in the first half of November, you are making a mistake.

Take more perspective. Trust that the revenue will land during the 4 days of BFCM.

Don't believe me?

Go to your ad account and compare revenue attributed at 1-day click / 7-day click / 28-day click.

On one client, the gap between 1DC and 28DC was 45% in August 2024. In November, it was 97%.

That's why, when you calculate the ROI of your advertising, don't think in days or weeks. Think of November as one whole unit.

Otherwise, you'll be making the wrong call — and leaving real money on the table.
