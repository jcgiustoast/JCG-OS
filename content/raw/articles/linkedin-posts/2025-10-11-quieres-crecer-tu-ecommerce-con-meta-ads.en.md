Want to grow your eCommerce with Meta Ads?

You don't just need creative volume. You need creative diversity.

If every ad you run points to the same buyer persona, you are capping your growth potential.

Say you sell matcha. If every ad targets women 20–30 with acidity issues, that is your ceiling — even if a much larger audience would benefit from the product.

Same thing if you lead with the product. The people who already know what matcha is and what it's for are a much smaller pool than the people looking for better sleep or a healthier alternative to coffee.

To unlock new levels of spend and growth on Meta, you need creative variety across every axis.

**By awareness level:**
- Unaware of the problem
- Problem-aware, not solution-aware
- Solution-aware, not product-aware
- Product-aware, not brand-aware
- Brand-aware, not yet a customer
- Existing customer

**By format and placement:**
- Reels, Feed, Stories, Threads
- Multiple aspect ratios and video lengths
- Different backgrounds, colors, and on-camera talent

**By buyer persona:**
- Demographic and psychographic segmentation
- Different purchase motivations
- Different price sensitivity

The more variety, the larger your addressable market and the further you can scale without losing efficiency.

Ideally, build a framework to systematically test which combinations work and which don't. (Happy to help with that.)
