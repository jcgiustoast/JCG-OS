I recently spoke with a founder who took his eCommerce from 0 to $50M in 24 months.

His top piece of advice? Focus.

He wasn't talking about having a great personal productivity routine. He meant a much more extreme kind of focus:

- 1 single product
- 1 single sales channel — Shopify
- 1 single paid channel — Meta
- 1 single country — the United States

No Amazon. No retail. No TikTok, no Pinterest. No new product lines.

To him, all of that was a waste of time — added operational complexity and spread the team's effort thin.

The obsession paid off:

- One of the best retention rates I've seen
- 500 new Meta creatives per month, all at very high quality
- Decisions that can be made from a single Shopify dashboard
- A lean, specialized, efficient team

The lesson: if your eCommerce is stuck, the answer might not be launching more products, more channels, or more countries.

What unlocks the next level might be doubling down on what you're already doing — more of it, and better.
